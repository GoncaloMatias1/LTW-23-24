The Washington Post

By Goncalo Matias, 15/2/2024

  • FC Porto suffered their fourth defeat in the Portuguese Football League this Monday, losing 3-2 at Arouca on matchday 21, falling further behind in the race for the title.
  • After a goalless draw with Rio Ave, the Portistas fell to goals from Rafa Mujica (first minute), Cristo Gonzalez (30, from the penalty spot) and Jason (61), to no avail with goals from Evanilson (eight, from the penalty spot) and Francisco Conceição (86).
  • Sergio Conceicaos eleven, which ended up with 10 due to Fábio Cardosos dismissal (90+6 minutes), remained in third place with 45 points, against 52 for leaders Benfica and Sporting (one game less), while Arouca picked up their fourth consecutive win and climbed to seventh with 28.
